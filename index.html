Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors arena

Here you battle the computer/AI in a fierce battle of stone paper and scissors.

  • Step 1:Double-Click on your choice of icon of Rock, Paper or Scissors
  • Step 2:After you play the move,Click on the Next Round button which will proceed you to another round
  • Step 3:After you are done getting destroyed by the AI,You can use the Reset button to reset the scores
